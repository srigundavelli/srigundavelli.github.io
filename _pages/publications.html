SCONE Applicability for Wi-Fi Networks

Sri Gundavelli, et al. IETF Internet-Draft

This draft examines the applicability of SCONE signaling to Wi-Fi networks and defines how network-assisted throughput and congestion guidance can improve application performance in Wi-Fi environments.

Selected Standards and Publications

SCONE Applicability for Wi-Fi Networks

This draft examines the applicability of SCONE signaling to Wi-Fi networks and defines how network-assisted throughput and congestion guidance can improve application performance in Wi-Fi environments.

draft-gundavelli-scone-wifi-applicability

Authorization Framework for NS/EP Services

This draft defines an authorization framework for National Security and Emergency Preparedness (NS/EP) services, with focus on enabling priority communications over IEEE 802.11 based access networks.

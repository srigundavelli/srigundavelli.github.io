Selected Standards and Publications

SCONE Applicability for Wi-Fi Networks

This draft examines the applicability of SCONE signaling to Wi-Fi networks and defines how network-assisted throughput and congestion guidance can improve application performance in Wi-Fi environments.

draft-gundavelli-scone-wifi-applicability

Authorization Framework for NS/EP Services

This draft defines an authorization framework for National Security and Emergency Preparedness (NS/EP) services, with focus on enabling priority communications across access networks. The work explores credential models, policy signaling, and authorization mechanisms that allow networks to recognize and prioritize eligible users and applications during emergency conditions.

draft-gundavelli-radepcs
